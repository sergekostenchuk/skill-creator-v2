# 10/10 Readiness Rubric

A release-grade skill should score at least 9/10 in every applicable category and at least 9.5/10 weighted average.

Categories:

- single-skill quality
- skill-group quality
- orchestrator-worker quality
- trigger quality
- regression preservation
- usability
- security and tool dependency quality
- simplicity and friction budget
- evidence integrity

No score should rely on planned commands, fake outputs, missing fixtures, or unevaluated impressions.
